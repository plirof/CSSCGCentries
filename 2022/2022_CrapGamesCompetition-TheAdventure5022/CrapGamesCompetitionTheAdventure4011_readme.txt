Introduction.
---------------
Have you ever wondered how it feels to be a judge in the CSSCGC?
Now live the thrills of being a judge in the CSSCGC from the safety of your home computer.
This is a Life simulator/RPG game where you control different characters in order to judge entries of different years.
Are you bored of games that are just copy/paste and minor modification from 2008 titles?

-------------
Instructions:
-------------
By selecting a year/judge you select different types of gameplay.
Each different judge have different skills (Inteligence, Charm, Strength ,Agility, Charisma & Crap awareness).

---------------
Main features :
---------------
-Realistic descriptions of the games!!
-HUGE game database. Almost all WOS games are described accuratelly in-game.
-The judge algorithm is the most advanced ever written-It is actually 3 years more advanced than the algorithm used in a game published in 2008 that has no relation -whatsoever with this game (hey Mr Lawer: "NO RELATION -WHATSOEVER" ).
-A team of professional judges were hired and then fired   just to make sure that the rules are implemented   perfectly & the game is realistic
-RPG style game as each Judge has its own unique characteristeics witch could cloud their judgement.
